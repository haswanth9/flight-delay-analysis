# Flight Delay Analysis (Data Analyst Project)

## Overview
This project analyzes U.S. flight delay data to identify patterns in delays, airline performance, and travel trends.

The goal is to help travelers understand:
- Best months to travel
- Most reliable airlines
- Busiest airports with delays

---

## Key Insights

### Best Months to Travel (Lower Delays)
- September
- November
- October
- February
- April

These months likely have lower demand and better prices.

---

### Worst Months to Travel (Higher Delays)
- June
- July
- August
- May
- December

These months are peak travel times → more delays.

---

### Most Reliable Airlines
- Delta (DL)
- United (UA)
- American (AA)

---

### Least Reliable Airlines
- SkyWest (OO)
- Southwest (WN)
